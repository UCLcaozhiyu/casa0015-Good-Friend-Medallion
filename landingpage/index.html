üìå A Bluetooth-based peer-to-peer location and direction finder

Inspired by the Witcher's medallion, this app uses Magic (BLE) to probe nearby Bluetooth devices and determine their distance. Not only can it help you find your friends, but it also acts as a powerful Bluetooth detector - revealing all hidden Bluetooth devices around you and their distances, leaving no Bluetooth signal unexposed!
